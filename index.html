LUONG QUOC VIET

ABOUT ME

Quoc Viet, Luong was born in Kon Tum City, Viet Nam. He graduated with a BE in Ho Chi Minh University of Technology in 2012. His major is Mechanical Engineering. He also graduated with a ME in Ho Chi Minh University of Technology in 2015. He is am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering, Korea Aerospace University, Goyang-si, Korea. The main goal of his research is to improve the performance of an intelligent landing gear equipped with an MR damper.
