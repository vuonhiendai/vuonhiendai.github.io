ABOUT ME

Quoc Viet, Luong was born in Kon Tum City, Viet Nam. He graduated with a BE in Ho Chi Minh University of Technology in 2012. His major is Mechanical Engineering. He also graduated with a ME in Ho Chi Minh University of Technology in 2015. He is am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the School of Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering, Korea Aerospace University, Goyang-si, Korea. The main goal of his research is to improve the performance of an intelligent landing gear equipped with an MR damper.

Single Landing gear

Robust Adaptive Control for an Aircraft Landing Gear Equipped with a Magnetorheological Damper

This research proposes a new robust controller based on model reference sliding mode control and adaptive hybrid control to improve the efficiency of absorbing landing impact energy, not only considering the variables of aircraft weight and sink speed but also managing uncertainties, such as ambient temperature and passive damping coefficient.

Intelligent Control Based on a Neural Network for Aircraft Landing Gear with a Magnetorheological Damper in Different Landing Scenarios

This paper adopts a neural network controller trained by two different methods, which are genetic algorithm and policy gradient estimation, for aircraft landing gear with an MR damper that considers different landing scenarios.
